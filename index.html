About Us

Who We Are

Scoko Initiative LLP is an investment-led organization committed to redefining healthcare through longevity science, wellness innovation, and alternative medicine.

We believe healthcare should focus not only on treating illness, but on preventing disease, extending lifespan, and improving daily wellbeing.

Vision

To become a trusted investment platform that accelerates the future of longevity-focused healthcare.

Mission

To empower innovative wellness solutions that deliver sustainable health outcomes and long-term value.

"The future of medicine is not just about living longer, but living better."
